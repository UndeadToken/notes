Natural Numbers: whole positive numbers.
Integers: whole positive or negative numbers.
Prime number: "Unbreakable" can only factored into a product of itself and 1
Prime factors: any nonprime natural number factors
Composite number: The product of prime factors
Perfect square: A natural number multiplied by itself
identity element:
  0 for addition (can add or subtract to any value without changin its value)
  1 for multiplication and divistion
Rational number: denominator is non zero, R represents all rational numbers that can exist
