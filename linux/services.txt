systemctl list-units - List units
systemctl status SERVICE(optional)

Service files typically reside within /run/systemd/system/ or /etc/systemd/system/.
Each can be modified with service-name.servoce.d/* directories.

e.g. ssh service - /lib/systemd/system/ssh.service

Target units precedence: default.target, graphical.target, multi-user.target

Attach target dependencies: /etc/systemd/system/target-name.target.wants/
