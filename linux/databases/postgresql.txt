PostgreSQL listens on TCP port 5432 of the local-host interface and on file-based socket /var/run/postgresql/.s.PGSQL.5432

Configuration file: postgresql.conf

Cluster config: /etc/postgresql/version/cluster-name/

Clusters are assigned nexta available port i.e 5433 ...

systemctl start postgresql

pg_hba.conf defines who is allowed to connect on each socket.
