Scilla (Smart Contract Intermediate Level Language)

Zilliqa is a sharded blockchain with high throughput and lower gas fees.

Scilla is Zilliqa's Smart Contract intermediate level language. Scilla is a formally verifiable 'Safe' Contract Language. Uses functional programming design principles (as in OCaml).

Zilliqa has a large ecosystem of dApps including unstoppable domains.

Smart Contracts
- Unlike traditional software, thet can't be updated
- They have a gas mechanism to pay for computational costs
