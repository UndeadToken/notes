Transitions, procedures

- Defines business logic, including state changes
- Implicit args _send, _amount, +user-defined args
- No return values, but can emit events, messages, errors

Transitions:
Can be called outside by other user accounts and contracts.

transition foo (vname_1 : vtype_1)

end

send msgs; {* another contract *}

Procedures:
Internal to the contract, can be called within transitions but not outside.

procedure foo (vname_1 : vtype_1)

end

foo v1 v2;
