resources:
https://ubuntu.com/blog/how-to-sign-things-for-secure-boot

Generate keys:
./make

Enroll key into shim:
sudo mokutil --import MOK.der

Check key exists:
sudo cat /proc/keys

Sign module:
kmodsign sha512 MOK.priv MOK.der module.ko

Check module was signed:
hexdump -Cv module.ko | tail -n 5

Insert module:
insmod module.ko
