Pre-engagement Interactions
Discuss scope & terms of the penetration test with the client to identify the goals of the engagement. Convey what can be expected from a full-scope penetration test without restrictions.

Intelligence Gathering
Collect information about the organization your attacking by using social media, google and footprinting.

Threat Modelling
Identify any vulnerabilities.

Vulnerability Analysis
Consider how to attack the target.

Exploitation
Launch well researched exploits that are garaunteed to succeed.

Post Exploitation
Persistence applications and noting scale of damage to organization brand, finances etc...

Overt Penetration Testing
The organization provides access to systems in which will be examined.

Covert Penetration Testing
No assumed knowledge is given about an organization. Reconnaisance, threat moddeling etc will be required to gain an understanding of the systems in place.
