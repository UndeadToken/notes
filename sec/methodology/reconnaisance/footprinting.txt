Gathering intelligence on a given target.

Passing footprinting:
hands off, public info search. e.g google, social media.

Active Footprinting:
Interaciton with out target e.g. port scan, talking to employees..

Benefits of footprinting:
Know security posture - types of software, operating systems, web servers etc.. 
Reduce focus area.
Identify vulnerabilities
Network map - figure out what to hack.
